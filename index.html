Cities We Serve

Discover amazing destinations across India

Varanasi

Experience the spiritual heart of India, where ancient ghats line the sacred Ganges River and timeless rituals unfold daily.

Puri

Visit the famous Jagannath Temple and relax on the golden beaches of this revered coastal pilgrimage town in Odisha.

Ujjain

Home to the Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga, Ujjain is a sacred city on the Shipra River, famous for the grand Kumbh Mela.

Ayodhya

Explore the legendary birthplace of Lord Rama, a city rich in history and devotion, highlighted by the magnificent Ram Mandir.

Haridwar

Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri in this holy gateway city where the Ganges enters the plains.
